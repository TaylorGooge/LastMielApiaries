About Us

Last Miel Apiaries is a small urban beefarm and garden located in downtown Tallahasee, Florida. We began beekeeping because of a fascination with the European Honey Bee and their behavior. We produce nucleus colonies, honey, beeswax, propolis, and pollen. Everything for sale in our store was produced locally right here in Tallahassee.
Our hives are registered with and inspected by the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services.
